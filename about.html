About Us

Welcome

The Electro Store story began in 2022, in an apartment, with 3 employees and 1 order (Sound system) delivered with 2 Dacia cars. We knew then that it is essential to anticipate the customers’ needs and pay attention to their every visit on the website. We built the platform with this state of mind. We developed constantly, we diversified the offer for our clients by adding new categories: Strong Sound System, Weak Sound System, Amplifiers, Cables for amplifiers, we increased our team and we learned a lot.

We are here to offer our customers what matters for their lives, saving them both time and money. We know that only together we will succeed, working as a team to find simple solutions, in order to create the best shopping experience. We are not afraid to say we strive for excellence. We work with destinations and goals to reach, but we also like to have fun. From the moment we become part of the organization’s life, we enter a race for speed, innovation and development. It’s a team journey just as it is an individual one.
